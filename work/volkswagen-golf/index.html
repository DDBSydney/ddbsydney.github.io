Volkswagen

Can we sell a Golf to a town that doesn't need its city technology?

So what was the brief then?

The job was to raise the consideration of the new model Golf, boost understanding of its advanced tech features and deliver more test-drives to Volkswagen dealers.

An idea that was destined to fail...

We developed an online series about a Volkswagen dealer given an impossible task. The premise was to sell the ultimate city car in an outback town filled with folks with no want or need for it.

Just as we expected...

Our rep Tom, made a grand total of zero sales in the town but on Facebook where the series was shown, viewers loved every awkward minute.

Facebook becomes a broadcast channel

We turned Facebook into our broadcast channel, so we could directly target potential buyers with the 6-month series.
