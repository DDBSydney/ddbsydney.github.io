Our culture

Celebrating our humanity in all its diversity lies at the heart of DDB's creative culture.

It goes back to Bill Bernbach’s policy of hiring ‘nice and talented’ people.

It defines the way we behave and the way we treat each other. And in a very old human tradition we have a list of 10 behaviours to remind us – after all, to err is human.

No. 1

We don’t pretend to be perfect. We don’t know it all, we strive to learn more.

No. 2

We see each other’s strengths rather than pick at weaknesses.

No. 3

We talk each other up, never down.

No. 4

We don’t blame, or take all the credit – we share responsibility for the highs and lows.

No. 5

We try to be honest and authentic.

No. 6

We speak as human beings – no jargon, no bullshit.
