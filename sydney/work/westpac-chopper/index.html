Westpac Chopper

We need a short punchy line to click through from homepage

To establish the new identity ‘Proud supporters of you’ we leveraged the brand’s key sponsorship program – the Westpac Lifesaver Rescue Helicopter.

Through an epic, single-take, the film tells the story of a man and his son floating helplessly in the ocean beside their upturned yacht. They drift, totally at the mercy of the elements, only to be plucked from danger by the arrival of the chopper.

Meanwhile, a voiceover expresses the key message that Westpac is there for all Australians, regardless of who they bank with.

In just two weeks 80% of people could spontaneously attribute the campaign to Westpac and best of all, it resonated most with our toughest audience, millennials.
