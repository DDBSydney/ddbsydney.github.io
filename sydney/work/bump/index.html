Bump

*need a short punchy line to click through from homepage*

To establish the new identity ‘Proud supporters of you’ we leveraged the brand’s key sponsorship program – the Westpac Lifesaver Rescue Helicopter.

The Task

Encourage the next generation to open their first bank account with Westpac.

The Insight

Stats say you’re more likely to get divorced than change bank accounts, so it’s vital to start a relationship with customers from an early age.

The Solution

On January 1, 2017, we created a new kind of bank account, only exclusively available to unborn babies.

The Bump Account is a savings account open to all babies born in 2017.

But rather than being a short-term grab for new customers, the idea was to set the next generation of kids up for the future – so every account came with a $200 deposit, which can’t be accessed until the child’s 16th birthday.

Family and friends were encouraged to set up regular contributions on birthdays and at Christmas, as we showed how much small deposits could add up to over time, allowing them to save for university, a house deposit or a world trip.

The results were phenomenal, in just the first week alone: - In one week the campaign generated 1.5million in PR. - 16,220 accounts had been registered. - 58% of those being non-Westpac customers.
