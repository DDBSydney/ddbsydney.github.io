Volkswagen

How we got Australia talking about a mystery prototype that didn’t exist.

The Problem

The new Volkswagen Amarok V6 is the most powerful pick-up truck in its class. But Aussies blokes couldn’t look past its European styling and see it for the class-leading truck that it was.

The Solution

If Aussie drivers were going to judge our truck by its cover we had to change the cover.

1. CREATING THE PROTOTYPE
So we built a completely new shell for the Amarok V6 that made it look as mean as the V6 engine that powered it.

2. LEAKING THE PROTOTYPE
Next, we leaked spy shots of the 'mystery prototype' to editorial media channels, igniting excitement and debate amongst performance enthusiasts.

3. TESTING THE PROTOTYPE
While the debate continued, we invited four unsuspecting performance enthusiasts to put the prototype to the test. With no preconceptions holding them back, the drivers were amazed by its raw power.

4. REVEALING THE PROTOTYPE
We then revealed the identity of the prototype by retargeting our audience online, changing the conversation from a powerful prototype to the powerful Amarok V6.

Not only did we announce the launch of the new Amarok V6, generating a significant amount of intrigue and excitement but we also sold some utes. The Amarok V6 model made up 38% of the Amarok sales in the first month and has broken Amarok sales records every month since launch
