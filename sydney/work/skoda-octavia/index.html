ŠKODA Octavia

For those that buy the car, not the ad.

The Problem

As a relative newcomer to the Australian automotive landscape the ŠKODA Octavia lacked awareness, it did however deliver on quality, design and engineering.

How could we get car buyers to open their eyes to the ŠKODA Octavia?

The Solution

We set about calling out all the traditional car advertising clichés, challenging buyers to look behind the curtain.
What they discovered was a brand determined to sell them a quality car vs. a clichéd image.

The Results

Sales for the Octavia have continued to strengthen since the campaign launched but more importantly awareness and saliency of the brand has a had a noticeable uplift.
