Volkswagen

Tech surprising enough to help mend a relationship

When is a car more than a car? When its technology is surprising enough to help mend a relationship.

Volkswagen has built on its successful ‘More than a car’ brand platform, launching a new ad via DDB Sydney that depicts a situation all too familiar for young couples, and how the Volkswagen’s App Connect features can help. The new spot by DDB Sydney is part of a series of three to be launched over the coming months.

It’s not technology itself that makes a Volkswagen More Than a Car. It’s the unexpected benefits and the stories that accompany them that makes a Volkswagen a character in our lives, and not just a means of getting from A to B.
