McDonalds

Macca’s fries are as iconic as the red and yellow packaging they come in.

But even the most loved icons must continue to reinvent themselves to remain relevant.

Based on the insight that Aussies like to do things their own way and aren’t afraid to bend the rules, Macca’s personified their fries, showing them to be full of new life and flavour.

By Bringing Fries to Life

After a successful launch we had to generate a second burst of conversation, so we created a pop-up ‘Fries Only’ restaurant to celebrate the Macca’s icon and experiment with a few potential new flavours.

The unanimous fan favourite was Sweet Chilli and Sour Cream. This became the focus of our summer campaign.

Results

The Loaded Fries platform illustrates its ability to remain on the menu long term. Now let’s let the fries do the talking…
