McDonalds

McDonald’s was modifying their famous, golden Fries for the first time in their history.

Problem

How do we launch Macca’s new Loaded Fries in a
fun way that will get Aussies excited to try them?

Solution

Literally “Bring Fries to Life” (just like the range of sauces does)
through a combination of macro videography and the voices of a
handful of hilarious comedians.

The recording process

We assembled a group of hilarious actors and set them
loose in the recording booth with our scripts and direction.

Where were they seen?

Videos were shown on television and online, as well as special pre-roll
and cinema placements. These placements were supported by a range of
OOH and digital placements that drove people to the films.
